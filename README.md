# Superstore_Performance_Dashboard_using_Power-BI
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit, customers, and regional performance of a retail superstore with actionable business insights.

## Project Objective
- Analyze overall sales, profit, and order performance
- Identify top-performing and underperforming regions
- Understand customer segments and purchasing behavior
- Track monthly sales & profit trends
- Evaluate shipping modes and product sub-categories
- Calculate and visualize profit margin %

## Key KPIs in Dashboard

- Total Sales :	Overall revenue generated
- Total Orders :	Number of customer orders
- Total Customers :	Unique customers
- Total Profit :	Net profit
- Average Order Value :	Sales ÷ Orders
- Profit Margin % :	Profit ÷ Sales

## Charts Used
-	Donut Chart: Orders by Ship Mode
-	Bar Chart: Sales by Sub-Category
-	Line Chart: Monthly Sales & Profit Trend
-	Table Matrix: Region-wise & Sub-Category-wise Sales
-	Map Visual: Geographic sales distribution across the US

# Key Insights
-	Technology category contributes the highest sales and profit
-	West region leads in overall revenue
-	Standard Class shipping is the most used mode
 - The strong sales growth in 4th Quarter, indicating increased customer demand during the year-end period
-	Some sub-categories have high sales but low profit, indicating discount issues
-	Overall profit margin is ~12.5%, which is healthy but improvable
